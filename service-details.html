Free Roam Arena Details

Immersive Wireless Experience

Experience the ultimate freedom with our Free Roam Arena. Unlike traditional VR where you are tethered to a computer, our arena uses backpack PCs and inside-out tracking, allowing you and your friends to move naturally in a large shared space.

Dodge attacks, explore fantasy worlds, and high-five your teammates in the virtual world. Our arena supports up to 6 players simultaneously with games ranging from zombie shooters to cooperative puzzle solving.
