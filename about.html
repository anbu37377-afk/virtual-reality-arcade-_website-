Who We Are

Building the Future of Entertainment

Founded in 2024, VR Arcade started with a simple mission: to make high-end virtual reality accessible to everyone. We believe VR is not just a game, but a new way to experience stories, connect with friends, and explore the impossible.

What began as a small pop-up with two headsets has grown into the city's premier VR destination, featuring state-of-the-art free roam arenas, 4D simulators, and a library of over 100 experiences.
