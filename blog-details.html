Top 10 Scary VR Games for Halloween

By Admin Oct 24, 2026
Halloween is just around the corner, and we have curated a list of the most terrifying VR experiences available at our arcade. From jump scares to psychological horror, get ready to scream.

Virtual Reality horror is unlike anything you've experienced on a flat screen. The immersion is total. When you hear a floorboard creak behind you, you don't just push a button to turn around—you have to physically turn and face your fear.

1. Phasmophobia VR

Become a paranormal investigator in this 4-player co-op psychological horror. Use your actual voice to interact with ghosts through the Ouija Boards and Spirit Boxes. Warning: The ghosts can hear you too.

2. Resident Evil 4 VR

The classic survival horror game reimagined for VR. Exploring the eerie village and fighting off Ganados feels completely new when you're aiming your weapon with your own hands.

"The scariest moment is when you realize there is nowhere to hide, because you are actually IN the game."

VR Arcade Head of Gaming

Conclusion

Whether you are a hardened horror veteran or a newcomer looking for a thrill, our arcade has the perfect spooky experience for you this Halloween. Book a slot now and face your fears!
